# Designing for tvOS

Guidelines for designing apps and games for the Apple TV platform.

## Device Characteristics

- **Display:** Very large, high-resolution TV screen
- **Ergonomics:** Users typically sit 8+ feet away but may move around the room while interacting
- **Inputs:** Siri Remote, game controllers, voice (Siri), and companion apps on other devices
- **App interactions:** People get deeply immersed in long sessions (often hours); picture-in-picture is expected for following alternative apps/video simultaneously

## System Features to Integrate

- Integrating with the TV app (video playback)
- SharePlay
- Top Shelf
- TV provider accounts

## Best Practices

- **Siri Remote gestures:** Support powerful, delightful interactions through the fluid, familiar gestures people make with the Siri Remote
- **Focus system:** Embrace the tvOS focus system — gently highlight and expand onscreen items as people navigate, helping them know what to do and where they are at all times
- **Cinematic presentation:** Deliver beautiful, edge-to-edge artwork, subtle and fluid animations, and engaging audio — content must be clear, legible, and captivating from across the room
- **Multiuser support:** Make sign-in easy and infrequent, handle shared sign-in, and automatically switch profiles when people change the current viewer
